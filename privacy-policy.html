🐕 Dog Walker Tracker

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026  ·  Effective date: April 2026

Summary: Dog Walker Tracker is a personal business management tool for dog walkers. We collect only the data you enter to run your business. We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or share it with third parties except the infrastructure providers needed to operate the app.

1. Who We Are

Dog Walker Tracker is an independent mobile application developed for professional dog walkers to manage clients, bookings, invoices and monthly reporting. References to "we", "us" or "our" refer to the developer of Dog Walker Tracker.

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at the address listed in section 9.

2. What Data We Collect and Why

We collect only the data you voluntarily enter into the app. We do not collect data automatically beyond what is necessary to operate the service.

Account data

  • Email address and password — used to create and authenticate your account via Supabase Auth.

Business data you enter

  • Owner records — name, mobile number, email address, and home address of your dog-walking clients. Used solely to manage your client relationships and send WhatsApp invoice summaries.
  • Dog records — dog names, hourly rates, and owner assignments. Used to create bookings and calculate fees.
  • Bookings — dates, times, services, fees, and notes for each walk. Used to display your calendar, generate monthly reports, and create invoices.
  • Calendar entries — miles driven per day. Used in monthly reporting.
  • Invoices — generated automatically from your booking data. Stored so you can track payment status and send summaries to clients.
  • Service types — walk service names and fees that you configure.
  • Non-working days — dates you mark as unavailable.

Notification preferences

  • Your email address for monthly report delivery, and your WhatsApp message template. Stored only to power features you explicitly enable.

3. How We Use Your Data

All data you enter is used exclusively to provide the features of Dog Walker Tracker:

  • Displaying your bookings, calendar, and monthly summaries
  • Generating and tracking invoices
  • Sending your monthly report to your chosen email address via Resend
  • Opening a pre-filled WhatsApp message to your clients (the message is composed on your device — we do not send WhatsApp messages on your behalf)
  • Generating PDF invoices for download or sharing

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, analytics, or any purpose other than operating the features you use.

4. Third Parties We Share Data With

We share data with the following service providers solely to operate the app. None of these parties use your data for their own purposes.

  • Supabase (supabase.com) — our database, authentication, and backend infrastructure provider. Your data is stored on Supabase's servers hosted in the EU (eu-west region). Supabase acts as a data processor under GDPR. See Supabase's Privacy Policy.
  • Resend (resend.com) — used to deliver your monthly report email. Your report email address is passed to Resend only when a report is sent. See Resend's Privacy Policy.

We do not share your data with any advertising networks, data brokers, analytics platforms, or any other third parties.

5. WhatsApp Integration

When you tap the WhatsApp send button on an invoice, the app opens WhatsApp on your device with a pre-composed message. The client's mobile number and the message text are passed to WhatsApp via a standard device link. We do not transmit this data to our servers or to WhatsApp independently. WhatsApp's own privacy policy governs what happens after you tap send in WhatsApp.

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in Supabase's EU (eu-west) region. Access is protected by:

  • Supabase Authentication — only you can access your account
  • Row Level Security — database rules ensure each user can only access their own data
  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) for all data in transit

We do not store any data locally on your device beyond what is temporarily cached by the app during a session.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active.

Deleting your account from within the app: You can permanently delete your account and all associated data directly inside Dog Walker Tracker. Go to Settings → Legal → Delete Account. You will be shown a summary of exactly what will be deleted, asked to enter your account password to verify your identity, and required to confirm you understand the action is irreversible. Once confirmed, the following is permanently and immediately removed from our systems:

  • All bookings, calendar entries, and walk history
  • All dog and owner records
  • All invoices and monthly reports
  • All service configurations and non-working days
  • All notification preferences and WhatsApp templates
  • Your authentication account (email and password)

This deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. There is no grace period or recovery option once confirmed.

You may also request deletion by contacting us at the address in section 9. We will permanently delete your account and all data within 30 days of a verified request.

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

As the app is operated from the UK and your data is stored in the EU, both UK GDPR and EU GDPR apply. You have the following rights:

  • Right of access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — correct inaccurate data (most data can be edited directly in the app)
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your account and all data
  • Right to data portability — request your data in a portable format
  • Right to object — object to any processing you believe is not justified

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address in section 9. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Dog Walker Tracker is intended for professional use by adults. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under the age of 16. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
