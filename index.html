Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026  ·  Effective date: April 2026

Summary: Dog Walker Tracker is a personal business management tool for dog walkers. We collect only the data you enter to run your business, plus optional GPS location data if you choose to enable the Beacon safety feature. We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or share it with third parties except the infrastructure providers needed to operate the app.

1. Who We Are

Dog Walker Tracker is an independent mobile application developed for professional dog walkers to manage clients, bookings, invoices, monthly reporting, and personal safety during walks. References to "we", "us" or "our" refer to the developer of Dog Walker Tracker.

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at the address listed in section 11.

2. What Data We Collect and Why

We collect only the data you voluntarily enter into the app, plus optional location data if you enable the Beacon feature. We do not collect data automatically beyond what is necessary to operate the service.

Account data

  • Email address and password — used to create and authenticate your account via Supabase Auth.

Business data you enter

  • Owner records — name, mobile number, email address, home address, and any optional fields you choose to enable (such as emergency contact details or consents). Used solely to manage your client relationships and send WhatsApp invoice summaries.
  • Dog records — dog names, hourly rates, owner assignments, and any optional fields you choose to enable (such as breed, date of birth, or notes). Used to create bookings and calculate fees.
  • Bookings — dates, times, services, fees, and notes for each walk. Used to display your calendar, generate monthly reports, and create invoices.
  • Calendar entries — miles driven per day. Used in monthly reporting.
  • Invoices — generated automatically from your booking data. Stored so you can track payment status and send summaries to clients.
  • Service types — walk service names and fees that you configure.
  • Non-working days — dates you mark as unavailable.

Notification and feature preferences

  • Your email address for monthly report delivery, your WhatsApp message template, cancellation policy settings, and field visibility preferences. Stored only to power features you explicitly enable.

Beacon location data NEW

🔵 Beacon — Optional Safety Feature

Beacon is an opt-in feature that must be explicitly enabled in Settings before it collects any data. No location data of any kind is collected unless you both enable Beacon in Settings and manually start a safety session.

When you start a Beacon safety session, we collect:

  • GPS location coordinates — latitude, longitude, accuracy, speed, and heading, recorded approximately every 20 seconds while the session is active (every 60 seconds or 75 metres when the app is in the background).
  • Session timestamps — the time your session started and ended.
  • A unique share token — a randomly generated link that allows your chosen emergency contact to view your live route in a web browser. This token is not linked to your name or email address.
  • Emergency contact details — name and mobile number of one trusted contact you choose to store. Used only to pre-fill the SMS composer when you share your safety link.

Location data is recorded only during sessions you manually start and stop. No background location tracking occurs at any other time.

3. How We Use Your Data

All data you enter is used exclusively to provide the features of Dog Walker Tracker:

  • Displaying your bookings, calendar, and monthly summaries
  • Generating and tracking invoices
  • Sending your monthly report to your chosen email address via Resend
  • Opening a pre-filled WhatsApp message to your clients (the message is composed on your device — we do not send WhatsApp messages on your behalf)
  • Generating PDF invoices for download or sharing
  • Beacon: Recording your GPS route during an active safety session, making your live location accessible via your unique share link, and pre-filling an SMS to your emergency contact with that link.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, analytics, or any purpose other than operating the features you use.

4. Third Parties We Share Data With

We share data with the following service providers solely to operate the app. None of these parties use your data for their own purposes.

  • Supabase (supabase.com) — our database, authentication, and backend infrastructure provider. All data including Beacon location points is stored on Supabase's servers hosted in the EU (eu-west region). Supabase acts as a data processor under GDPR. See Supabase's Privacy Policy.
  • Resend (resend.com) — used to deliver your monthly report email. Your report email address is passed to Resend only when a report is sent. See Resend's Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub Pages (github.com) — hosts the Beacon share page (dog-walkertracker.github.io/dwt-beacon). When your emergency contact visits your share link, the page is served by GitHub Pages. The page then fetches your session data directly from Supabase using your anonymous share token. GitHub Pages does not have access to your personal data. See GitHub's Privacy Statement.

We do not share your data with any advertising networks, data brokers, analytics platforms, or any other third parties.

5. Beacon — Location Tracking Detail 🔵 Beacon

When tracking occurs

Location tracking only occurs when all three of the following are true:

  • You have enabled Beacon in Settings
  • You have granted location permission to the app
  • You have manually tapped "Start Safety Session"

Tracking stops immediately when you tap "Stop Session". No location data is collected outside of active sessions.

Background location

When a Beacon session is active, the app may continue recording your location when it is running in the background or your screen is locked. This is necessary so your emergency contact always has your most recent position. A blue indicator bar appears on your iPhone's status bar whenever background location is active. If you force-quit the app, background tracking will stop.

Battery-saving behaviour

The app uses your phone's motion sensor to automatically pause location recording when you are stationary, and switches to a reduced frequency (every 90 seconds) when your battery level drops below 20%. This means there may be gaps in the recorded route during stationary periods.

Who can see your location

Your live route is accessible only to anyone who has your unique Beacon share link. You control who receives this link — it is only shared when you tap "Send Safety Link" and choose to send it via SMS or another messaging app. The link contains a 32-character random token and is not guessable. It does not contain your name, email, or any other identifying information.

Share link expiry

Your share link remains accessible until the session ends or the underlying data is deleted (see section 7). After 90 days, the location data is deleted and the share link will show no route data.

6. WhatsApp Integration

When you tap the WhatsApp send button on an invoice, the app opens WhatsApp on your device with a pre-composed message. The client's mobile number and the message text are passed to WhatsApp via a standard device link. We do not transmit this data to our servers or to WhatsApp independently. WhatsApp's own privacy policy governs what happens after you tap send in WhatsApp.

7. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in Supabase's EU (eu-west) region. Access is protected by:

  • Supabase Authentication — only you can access your account
  • Row Level Security — database rules ensure each user can only access their own data
  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) for all data in transit

Beacon location data is additionally protected by the share token model — location points can only be retrieved if you know the session's unique 32-character token.

We do not store any data locally on your device beyond what is temporarily cached by the app during a session.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active, with the following specific rules for Beacon data:

Beacon location data retention 🔵 Beacon

  • Location points (GPS coordinates) are automatically deleted after 90 days from the date they were recorded.
  • Session records (start time, end time, share token) are retained until your account is deleted.
  • Emergency contact details are retained until you edit or delete them, or until your account is deleted.

You can stop any active session at any time from the Beacon screen, which immediately ceases all further location recording for that session.

Deleting your account

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data directly inside Dog Walker Tracker. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. You will be shown a summary of exactly what will be deleted, asked to enter your account password, and required to confirm the action is irreversible. Once confirmed, the following is permanently and immediately removed:

  • All bookings, calendar entries, and walk history
  • All dog and owner records
  • All invoices and monthly reports
  • All service configurations and non-working days
  • All notification preferences and WhatsApp templates
  • All Beacon sessions, location points, and emergency contact details
  • Your authentication account (email and password)

This deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. There is no grace period or recovery option once confirmed.

You may also request deletion by contacting us at the address in section 11. We will permanently delete your account and all data within 30 days of a verified request.

9. Your Rights (GDPR)

As the app is operated from the UK and your data is stored in the EU, both UK GDPR and EU GDPR apply. You have the following rights:

  • Right of access — request a copy of the data we hold about you, including any Beacon location history
  • Right to rectification — correct inaccurate data (most data can be edited directly in the app)
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your account and all data, including location history
  • Right to data portability — request your data in a portable format
  • Right to object — object to any processing you believe is not justified
  • Right to withdraw consent — you can disable Beacon at any time in Settings, which prevents any further location data collection

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address in section 11. We will respond within 30 days.
